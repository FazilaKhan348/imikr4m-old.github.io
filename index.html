Biography

I am a PostDoctoral research scholar in Censored Planet at University of Michigan. Before joining Censored Planet, I did my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at The University of New South Wales, under the supervision of Prof. Mohamed Ali (Dali) Kaafar. My current research interests are focused on data-driven analysis of privacy and security issues in networked systems.
